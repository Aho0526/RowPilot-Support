4. Information We Collect

The App may collect the following information:

  • Location Data (GPS): to record and display practice distance, speed, pace, and route.
  • Date and Elapsed Time
  • Device Information (OS type, etc., for operation verification)

The information collected is limited to what is necessary to provide the features of the App. Except when using the Team Management Feature (Section 6.1), all collected information is processed locally within the App and is not transmitted to external servers.

5. Handling of Location Data

The App utilizes location data (GPS) for the purpose of recording practice distance, speed, and route. Location data is collected only if the user permits it in the device settings.

The collected location data will be used only within the scope of providing the features of the App. We do not view, collect, or provide this information to third parties. Users can stop the use of location data at any time through their device settings.

6. Data Storage

Practice record data is saved on the user's device. Data may be lost due to device change, app deletion, etc. However, backup to iCloud enables preservation of information. We are not responsible for any loss or corruption of data.

6.1 Team Management Feature

For recording in school clubs or organizations, the RowPilot Team plan and above include a feature that allows batch saving of multiple users' records. The records of users belonging to a team are automatically transmitted to the Cloudflare D1 data server and shared among team members.

The record data transmitted and saved in this process includes the following:

  • User-configured name
  • Session summary
  • Records

Note that these features are only active for data connected to Concept2 PM5 in the practice tab. Outdoor workout results will not be transmitted or saved.

6.2 Data Storage (Storage Using iCloud)

Personal records, including indoor and outdoor workouts, can be saved to the user's iCloud account at their own option. Since this backup data includes on-the-water workout records, location data is also saved. However, because it is encrypted, neither the developer nor Apple can access this data.

8.1 SOS Feature

The App is equipped with an SOS feature designed to assist in responding to emergencies on the water.
*Note: This feature cannot be used unless a name and contact phone number are registered in the settings.*

By pressing and holding the button on the screen for 1.5 seconds and tapping the displayed SMS send button, an SMS containing location data, name, battery level, and time will be sent to the registered contact. Please verify the operation of this feature before actual use.

The SOS feature is an auxiliary function and does not replace emergency call methods to police, fire departments, etc. We shall not be liable for any damages caused by operational delay, non-operation, or failure to deliver notifications of this feature.
