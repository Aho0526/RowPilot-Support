Important Notice

Functional Restrictions & Auto-Cleanup
on Team Plan Migration

Starting from v1.0.1, the behavior when a subscription plan is changed after creating a team (such as a downgrade or expiration due to non-renewal), which was previously undefined, has been clarified.
A control flow has been officially introduced to prevent unauthorized continuation of use and to properly manage service resources.

Effective Date: July 4, 2026

Implementation Date: July 7, 2026 (v1.0.1 (Build 1))

On RowPilot, when a change is made to your subscription plan (such as Team, MAX, or Organization), the following synchronization, restriction, and auto-cleanup flow will apply. Please check the details according to your migration type.

1. Migration to a Higher Team Plan (e.g., Team → MAX / Org)

When migrating to a plan that supports more members, the plan change is automatically reflected as follows:

Immediate Sync

Plan Synchronization & Limit Expansion

The team's subscription status will automatically synchronize in the background, and the maximum limit for member additions will expand based on the new plan immediately.

2. Migration to a Lower Team Plan (e.g., Org → MAX / Team)

If you change to a team plan with a lower member limit, and the number of existing members at the time of migration exceeds the new plan's limit, the following adjustment flow applies:

Day 1 〜 Day 30

1-Month (30-Day) Grace Period & Warnings

A 30-day grace period starts immediately after migration. During this period, a warning banner will be displayed at the top of the team management screen, and a "Deletion Scheduled" warning label will be shown next to the members targeted for auto-deletion (surplus members with newer creation times, created_at).

* "Day 1" is not the day you requested the plan change, but the day your current plan's subscription period ends and the new plan officially takes effect. Your current member limit applies until the end of the subscription period.

During Grace Period

Manual Adjustment by Administrator

The admin (owner) can adjust the target members by manually kicking unnecessary members. Once the member count is within the new limit, the warning and scheduled auto-deletion will be cancelled automatically.

After Day 30 (Upon Access)

Auto-Removal of Exceeding Members

If the member count is not reduced to the limit by the deadline, surplus members (starting from the newest accounts) will be automatically removed (deleted) from the team upon the next access after the deadline.

3. Migration to a Non-Team Plan (e.g., Org → Pro / Free)

If you change to an individual plan (Pro) or a free plan (Free) that does not support team creation, or if your subscription expires, the following archival and data protection measures apply:

Day 1 〜 Day 30

30-Day Archival Grace Period

A 30-day grace period is set upon plan change detection. During this period, all team features (including uploading workouts) remain fully accessible without restrictions.

* "Day 1" is not the day you requested the plan change, but the day your current plan's subscription period ends and the downgrade or expiration officially takes effect. You can continue using team features until the end of the subscription period.

After Day 30

"Archived (Read-Only)" State Applied

After the 30-day grace period, the team enters an "Archived (Read-Only)" state.

Along with this, the roles of all team members (except the owner) will be changed to viewer.

Archived Restrictions:
• Team members are blocked from uploading new workouts. A message: "This team is archived. Please contact the team administrator." will be displayed.
• Team management controls (e.g., name changes, code regeneration, member approvals, role changes) are locked (disabled).
• Team data is NOT deleted: Unlike the previous policy, team data and workout records will not be deleted, and you can access and browse past records at any time.

Cancelling the Archived State:
If you re-register or migrate to a plan that supports team creation during the 30-day grace period or after archiving, the archived state will be cancelled automatically, returning the team to normal operation.

Inquiries

If you have any questions regarding this policy change or the handling of data after plan migration, please feel free to contact our support desk at rowpilot.jp@gmail.com.
