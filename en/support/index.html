FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Please check here first if you encounter any issues.

Bluetooth & Connection
I cannot connect to Concept2 PM5 via Bluetooth. ▼

Please try the following steps in order:

  1. Go to **"Connect" -> "Wireless"** on your PM5 monitor menu to turn on wireless communication.
  2. Ensure RowPilot has Bluetooth permission enabled in iOS Settings -> Privacy & Security -> Bluetooth.
  3. Start Bluetooth scanning in the Practice tab, and the PM5 should appear in the device list.
  4. If the connection is unstable, temporarily turn off or move away from other nearby Bluetooth devices.
  5. Make sure your PM5 monitor is updated to the latest firmware (accessible from the PM5 menu).

If the issue persists, please contact us via email.

How do I connect multiple ergs simultaneously? ▼

Connecting multiple ergs simultaneously is available on the Manager, Team, MAX, and Organization plans (up to 10 ergs). Note that 1-on-1 PM5 connection is available on the Free and Pro plans as well.

After turning on Bluetooth on each PM5 individually, start scanning in the Practice tab of RowPilot to see them in the list. Tap each device to connect sequentially. You can also batch-transmit the same menu to all connected ergs.

Data & Backup
Where is my data saved? ▼

RowPilot data is primarily saved in your local device storage. If you enable team synchronization on the Team plan or above, your team's training statistics will also be saved on our secure external database (Cloudflare D1).

Uninstalling the app will delete local device data. We highly recommend exporting your valuable records regularly using the app's built-in export feature.

How do I transfer data after changing devices? ▼

RowPilot primarily uses local device storage for data, so reinstalling or changing devices will not automatically restore your records. Please make sure to export your records before switching devices.

If you are using team synchronization on the Team plan or above, logging in with the same Apple ID will automatically sync your team data.

I want to delete my recorded data. ▼

Individual training sessions can be deleted from the History tab in the app by swiping or long-pressing.

If you wish to delete all data completely, simply uninstall the app. (To delete team data uploaded to the sync server, please use the team data deletion feature inside the app's settings.)

Features & Usage
How do I setup and use the SOS feature? ▼

Preparation: Go to the "Settings" tab -> "SOS Contacts" and pre-register the email addresses or phone numbers of your emergency contacts.

Usage: Tap the SOS button on the RowMode screen (GPS rate meter) to instantly send a message containing your current GPS coordinates to your registered contacts. We highly recommend setting this up before heading out on the water.

Note: SOS sending uses the native iOS Mail or Messages app. Location permissions must be granted to RowPilot to retrieve coordinates.

How do I change the location on TideView? ▼

Tap the location name at the top of the TideView screen to open the list. You can search and select from 239 ports/prefectures across Japan. The tide graph will update instantly. You can bookmark frequently used training spots.

RowMode's stroke detection is not working properly. ▼

Adjust the motion detection threshold in the "Settings" tab -> "Sensor Sensitivity". The optimal values vary depending on the force of your strokes and the mounting direction of your iPhone.

  • If sensitivity is too high: Raise the threshold (reduces false detections from noise).
  • If sensitivity is too low: Lower the threshold (detects weaker strokes).

Securing the iPhone directly to the boat rack or hull will significantly improve accuracy.

Subscription
What happens to my data if I change or cancel (downgrade) my team plan? ▼

Upon plan migration, an auto-cleanup flow and functional restrictions will apply to prevent unauthorized data usage and manage team resources properly.

If the number of members exceeds the limit of your new plan, a 30-day grace period will be applied. If you migrate to a non-team plan (Pro/Free), the team will enter a "Suspended (Read-Only)" state after a 14-day grace period, and team data will be scheduled for deletion 1 month after the plan change.

For detailed restrictions and cancellation options, please refer to our notice page:

Team Plan Migration Policy (Details) →

How do I cancel the Premium plan? ▼

Open iOS Settings -> tap your Apple ID at the top -> Subscriptions -> RowPilot -> Cancel Subscription.

If you cancel before the billing cycle ends, you can continue using the premium features for the remainder of the month. Your data will not be lost.

How do I restore my purchase? (After changing devices or reinstalling) ▼

Go to Settings -> Subscriptions -> tap "Restore Purchase" inside the app. As long as you are signed in with the same Apple ID used for purchase, your subscription will be recognized automatically.

If you cannot restore, please verify that you are signed in with the correct Apple ID in iOS Settings -> App Store.

Can I share my plan via Family Sharing? ▼

Currently, App Store Family Sharing is not supported. If you want to use the app with a team, please use the manager sharing feature available in the Team, MAX, and Organization plans. One coach/club can purchase the plan and share manager mode access with crew members.

Others
How are the revenues donated to the Kochi Rowing Association? ▼

Once a year, the developer officially donates the revenue—excluding the App Store fees (30% or 15%), taxes, and minimal server maintenance costs—to the Kochi Rowing Association. The funds are used to maintain rowing equipment and support junior and youth athletes.

Where can I see the open-source code? ▼

We publish it to ensure data management transparency. Note that all copyrights belong to the developer, and unauthorized secondary usage is strictly prohibited.
