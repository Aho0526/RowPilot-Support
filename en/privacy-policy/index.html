Privacy Policy

RowPilot is designed to keep data handling as simple as possible. This page describes the permissions required by the app, the types of data collected, storage locations, and policies on sharing with third parties.

Last Updated: June 28, 2026

⚠️
Available Only in Japan RowPilot is currently distributed exclusively on the App Store in Japan. Additionally, features like tide forecasts and weather data are optimized only for locations within Japan.

1. Developer Access to Data

RowPilot is designed around on-device processing. Your training data and settings are managed on your device or in your personal cloud space.

Training records, location, settings, and contacts are stored locally. Except for the team synchronization feature, data is never automatically transmitted to developer-managed servers.

RowPilot's source code is publicly hosted on GitHub to ensure full implementation transparency.

2. Permissions Required and Their Purpose

RowPilot requests the following permissions from iOS. These permissions are used exclusively for their stated purposes.

Location Services (GPS)

Used for calculating speed and mapping routes in RowMode, and for generating SOS messages. Coordinates are collected only during active workouts and are not tracked in the background.

  • Collection timing: Only during active RowMode sessions
  • Storage: On-device
  • SOS activation: Sends location link directly to pre-configured emergency contacts via SMS
Bluetooth

Used for wireless connection to Concept2 PM5 ergs. Communication is contained entirely between your iPhone and the PM5 monitor.

  • Target device: Concept2 PM5 only
  • Data retrieved: Distance, time, stroke rate, pace, and heart rate
Motion & Fitness Sensors

Used for stroke rate detection in RowMode. Sensor data is processed only during active workouts.

  • Collection timing: Only during active RowMode sessions
Cloud Synchronization

When a team is created on a Team plan or above, and a member enables sync, training statistics are saved on our external server (Cloudflare D1). See Section 5 for details.

  • Target users: Users belonging to a team on Team, MAX, or Organization plans
  • Data recorded: Numerical training statistics and in-app member IDs
SOS Contacts

Manually entered emergency contacts (emails or phone numbers) are stored locally on the device. SOS messages are sent directly via SMS.

3. Data Storage and Management

The storage location and accessibility of data handled by RowPilot are as follows:

Data Type Storage Location Developer Access
Training records (water) On-device None
Training records (erg) On-device None
GPS coordinates & routes On-device None
SOS contacts On-device None
App settings On-device None
Team management data (Team or above) Cloudflare D1 (Stats only) Stats only*

* Only numerical training data (distance, time, rate, etc.) is saved on the sync server. GPS coordinates and route details are never uploaded. See Section 5 for details.

4. Data Sharing Between Users

RowPilot allows users to share workout records via Apple's **AirDrop** (P2P direct communication). Since no intermediary server is used, no data is stored on a third-party server.

AirDrop communications are protected by Apple's end-to-end encryption, and the receiver must explicitly approve each transfer.

5. Server Synchronization for Team Plans

Team, MAX, and Organization plans utilize an external database (Cloudflare D1) to synchronize records among members.

Data recorded on the server:

  • Training stats (distance, time, rate, pace, power, etc.)
  • In-app member IDs (name and email are not included)

Data NOT recorded on the server:

  • GPS coordinates and routes (location details are kept strictly on-device)
  • SOS contacts
  • Personal identification (Apple ID, email, etc.)

6. Purchase and Billing Processing

RowPilot Premium subscriptions are processed via Apple's StoreKit 2.

  • Billing information (credit cards, etc.) is secured and managed by Apple. The developer has no access to it.
  • The developer only receives purchase status (success/failure).
  • Subscription management and cancellation can be handled in App Store settings.
  • For payment disputes and refunds, please contact Apple Support directly.

7. Third-Party Services

Services currently used by RowPilot:

Japan Meteorological Agency Tide Data API

Retrieves tide prediction data for tide graphs. No personal information is included in these API calls.

Cloudflare, Inc. (Cloudflare D1)

Used as the sync database for Team, MAX, and Organization plans. See Section 5 for details.

Strava Integration (In Development)

We plan to offer Strava integration. This will require explicit user authorization (OAuth) and will never share data without consent.

We do not use advertising SDKs or tracking/analytics tools (such as Google Analytics or Firebase Analytics) to track user behavior.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy due to feature updates or regulatory changes.

  • Changes will be marked by updating the "Last Updated" date.
  • For major changes, we will notify users via in-app notifications or on our support page.
  • Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
