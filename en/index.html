Features

What RowPilot Can Do

Covering all features needed for both on-water and ergometer training. Elevate both rowers and managers with a single app.

Home Screen

Weather, tide times, rigging settings, monthly total distance, and recent sessions at a glance. Features a global search to find any data across the app instantly.

RowMode

High-precision stroke rate (SPM) automatic calculation via the iPhone's built-in accelerometer and GPS tracking. The UI automatically optimizes based on device orientation.

PM5 Connectivity (Practice Tab)

Bluetooth connection to Concept2 rowing ergometers (PM5). Supports workout menu synchronization (send/receive) and multi-erg training. (Requires Manager plan or above)

Rigging Management

Record boat and oar settings down to the millimeter. Built-in vector drawings linked with SwiftUI move dynamically based on input values, visualizing your setup intuitively.

Tide & Weather Forecasts

Based on the Japan Meteorological Agency's predictions, the app automatically identifies the nearest observatory from 239 locations across Japan based on GPS to show tide graphs. Weather conditions like wind speed and direction, critical for rowing, are also retrieved.

Emergency SOS

Instantly send your current location (with a map link) to registered emergency contacts in case of a capsize or injury.

The Story Behind RowPilot

A severe shortage of GPS stroke rate meters in our club—that was the starting point of development. Commercial units are expensive, and equipping everyone in the crew is difficult. Then I realized we could use the smartphones that everyone already owns. From that spark of inspiration, a one-year development journey began.

Initially, we planned a simple measurement app, but requests like "I want to see tide info in the app" or "I want to sync erg data" kept piling up. Before we knew it, it had evolved into an integrated app covering both on-water and off-water training.

"Connecting to Concept2 PM5 via Bluetooth was a real challenge. I hit a wall that couldn't be resolved even after discussing it with AI for days. Ready to give up, I sent an email to Concept2 headquarters, and their engineer, Ryan, kindly helped me out. Ultimately, we succeeded in sending synchronized workouts to 9 ergs and establishing stable two-way communication on 8 of them."

Part of the revenue is donated to the Kochi Rowing Association. I sincerely hope that this app, built by a high school student together with AI, can contribute to raising the bar of the rowing community.

Kaito Nakahira
